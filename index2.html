    • Consultation: We begin by discussing your invention and understanding your requirements. Whether you have rough sketches, CAD files, or just a concept, we will work with you to bring your vision to life.
    • Drafting: Our team of skilled drafters will create initial drafts based on your input, ensuring that every aspect of your invention is accurately represented.
    • Review: We will provide you with the drafts for review and feedback. Your input is crucial in ensuring that the final drawings meet your expectations and comply with patent office requirements.
